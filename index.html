Calculate Standing Reach

Your standing reach is calculated based on your height and wingspan. This is how high you can reach while standing flat-footed.

Determine Required Height

To dunk, you need to get the ball over the rim (10 feet). Depending on dunk type and hand size, you need 6-12 inches clearance above the rim.

Required Height = Rim Height + Clearance

Subtract your standing reach from the required height to find out how much vertical jump you need.

Vertical Needed = Required Height - Standing Reach
